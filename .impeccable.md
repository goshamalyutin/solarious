# Impeccable — Solarious

## Design Context

### Users

Crypto-literate investors, validators, and infrastructure operators evaluating an
energy-backed Layer-1 before TGE (June 2026). They read a marketing landing page
the way they read other serious L1 / infra sites (base.org, layerzero.network,
wormhole.com, polygon.technology): skeptical, fast-scanning, allergic to hype.
Job to be done: decide in 60 seconds whether this is real infrastructure worth a
whitelist signup, not a meme token.

### Brand Personality

Three words: **measured, physical, unimpressed.** The product's whole promise is
"every $SOLAR traces to a real kilowatt" — sell with facts (validator counts,
supply caps, kWh), never emotion. Voice rules are strict and enforced:

- No em-dashes. No "not X, but Y." No AI filler (delve, seamless, unlock,
  revolutionize, empower, game-changer).
- Short declarative statements. State a fact, a number, a consequence.
- Sentence-case headlines with a trailing period; UPPERCASE only for
  letter-spaced kickers.

### Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme: light only.** Warm pearl surfaces (`--pearl #E7DED8`,
  `--pearl-alt #EFE9E3`), ink text, footer on midnight `#0A0A0A`. Not negotiable.
- **One accent: orange `#F07501`, under 5% of pixels.** Exactly one orange word
  per headline. Never a second hue.
- **Type: Clash Display (headlines) + Geist (body/UI) + Geist Mono (labels/
  units/index markers).** Clash Display (Fontshare, self-hosted woff2 400/500/
  600/700) carries h1/h2/.h-mega only; h3/h4 and body stay on Geist. Clash is
  geometric and runs wide, so display tracking is relaxed (~-0.02em, never the
  old -0.03em choke). Geist alone read as the Vercel default; Clash gives the
  headlines distinct character without touching body legibility.
- **Hero headline motion: word-by-word blur-up** (framer-motion, stagger ~0.09s,
  ease [0.2,0.65,0.3,0.9]), reduced-motion safe. The LZ/Linear/Vercel entrance.
- **Reference for STRUCTURE, not color:** layerzero.network. Borrow its restraint,
  negative space, hairline-ruled rows, index markers (`[01]`, `///`), small
  confident type, hard left-alignment, and strict asymmetric grid. Translate all
  of that into the warm-pearl + single-orange skin. Do NOT copy its dark theme.
- **Anti-references / banned:** giant morphing-word gimmicks; four identical
  card-grids in a row; the big-number "hero metric" template; everything centered
  with uniform padding; glassmorphism as decoration; gradient text; colored
  side-stripe borders; neon/purple AI palettes.

### Design Principles

1. **Restraint beats decoration.** If an element is decoration, cut it. The light
   and the facts carry the page.
2. **Rows over cards.** Prefer hairline-ruled, index-marked list rows to repeated
   icon+heading+text card grids. Cards only when a thing is genuinely a card.
3. **Left-aligned and asymmetric.** Section heads left, content on a 12-col grid
   with generous, varied whitespace. Stop centering everything.
4. **One orange, one morphing idea per screen.** Accent is rare on purpose.
5. **Motion is atmosphere, not spectacle.** Max ~4 persistent animated elements,
   all respecting prefers-reduced-motion.
